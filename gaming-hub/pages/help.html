FAQ

Why is social sign-in not working yet?

Google and Apple require provider setup in Supabase and valid callback URLs.

How does registration count work?

It reads from a real profiles table in Supabase, not a hardcoded frontend number.

How do I replace hero images?

Update URLs in the image manifest file with your approved, licensed image links.
